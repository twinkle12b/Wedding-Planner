About us

Events Square! is an imaginative and innovative design & planning team focusing on the fresh, the fun, and the never been done!

Striking far beyond your average wedding planner, we aim to create and curate an affair that’s uniquely you. Like small batch…anything (scotch? fine French soap? cookies, perhaps? Yes, let’s go with cookies!) each event is made with love, passion and a hint of spice. We take pride in seeking the road less traveled, pulling inspiration from daily beauty and real life to create timeless and effortless experiences for couples and guests alike. Offering clients the best of the best, from selecting premier vendors, styling and directing photoshoots, custom event branding and more, a Events Square! wedding is full service at its finest.
